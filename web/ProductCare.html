Product Care

Care and Storage of Leather Ware
-Wipe the hardware with dry cloth frequently to keep it 
lustrous and avoid color deterioration.
- Wipe the leather ware with soft cloth lightly
- Pack the hardware parts
- Put in preservative and moth repellant
- Put in soft cloth / soft paper around the ware to make it 
shaped
- Put it into a dust-proof bag
- Hang the bag at a dry place
(No folding and no stacking for long)

How to Take Good Care of a Leather Bag
To maintain leather bag in good condition, the following 
points are highly recommended.
--Protect your leather bag from humidity and avoid exposure 
to sunlight or to any direct heat source as its shape will 
be easily deformed or the color may fade.
--Avoid overloading the bag.
--To avoid color migration, please do not put your leather bag 
into prolonged contact with other leathers.
--Avoid using plastic bag to store shinny leather bag.
--Avoid contacting with water and any product containing 
alcohol (perfume, solventsâ€¦), grease or abrasive products 
in general.

Daily Care
Simply daily care can lift up the durability of leather bag. 
--If a leather bag gets wet, use a dry cloth to absorb the 
moisture. Then air the bag in shaded area. Do not use dryer 
or air-conditioner, or expose under sunlight for drying. 
Otherwise, cracks on the bag may be easily caused.
--Use leather stain remover on regular basis. Leather stain 
remover should be placed on a cloth rather than on the 
leather surface directly. Do not wash the bag in water or 
with any unknown chemical solvents.
--Drying agents (e.g. Silica get) can be placed into 
leather bag when kept for a relative long period.
--In order to preserve your leather bag, please store them 
in the dust bag that was offered to you.

How to Clean a Leather Bag
Different kinds of leather require different cleaning 
methods to maintain their best condition.
--Calf & Sheep skin
Put leather stain remover on dry cloth to rub away dirt. 
Then, polish the whole bag to make it shine.
--Patent Leather & Metallic Leather
Patent leather should be cleaned with a soft dampened 
cloth, and then dried with a clean cloth.
--Woven & Nylon Material
Apply dilute soap or stain remover with water and then 
slightly rub away dirt marks with soft brush. Do not 
soak and clean the bag in the water.

How to Keep a Mal-function Zipper in Good Condition
--Put a small amount of white wax along the zipper to 
smoothen it.
--If gold, Silver or Bronze Metal Fitting is tarnished, please apply 
metal shine lotion on a cloth and rub the tarnished area gently 
until the metal becomes shinny.
